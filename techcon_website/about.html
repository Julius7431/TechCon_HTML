History

TechCon Systems, Inc. was Established in the year 1985 Located at Slidell, Louisiana. Specializes in structural designs and technical assistance, particularly in slab designs, utilizing advanced computer hardware and software.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower individuals to shape the future of technology by creating a platform that brings together visionaries, industry leaders, and tech enthusiasts to share ideas and insights. Committed to promoting diversity and inclusivity, TechCon ensures technology serves and represents everyone while driving sustainable solutions to global challenges through collective knowledge and action.

Past Speakers

Jane Smith: AI Pioneer and CTO at InnovateAI Dr. Jane Smith is a trailblazer in artificial intelligence and machine learning, leading groundbreaking research that transformed healthcare analytics. At TechCon, she shared insights on the ethical implications of AI in modern technology.

Elijah Gomez: Founder of CodeForGood Elijah Gomez is renowned for his contributions to open-source communities and his work on scalable technologies for non-profits. At TechCon, he inspired the audience with his talk on harnessing technology for social impact.
